About Me

My name is Hannah Van Opstal and I am an aspiring world-changer. Above all else, my dream is to leave the world with something positive, sustainable and beautifully-designed.

Currently, I am studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, but I also moonlight as a vinyl connoisseur, self-titled master chef, notebook artist and forest guide. I love trying new things, and feel like I am missing out when I don't. I thrive on stress (and coffee), and love to stay busy. Because of this, my calendar is almost always overflowing with exciting events like concerts, workshops and volunteering opportunities.

My main focuses lie in sustainable design, and helping those in need. I hope to one day create something that will improve the lives of people in need, maybe through a social program or green initiative. Whether it's collaborating with passionate and imaginative people, exploring new places, or just watching a movie with my friends, I am always moving forward and always up to something.

Over the next four months, I am planning to develop this website to create a digital masterpiece. As you can tell though, it is still undergoing massive developments so stay tuned for updates!
