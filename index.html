About Me

My name is Hannah Van Opstal and I am an aspiring world-changer. My ambition, above all else, is to leave the world with something positive, sustainable and hopefully beautifully-designed.

Although I am currently studying Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, I moonlight as a vinyl connoisseur, self-titled master chef, notebook artist and forest guide. I love trying new things, and feel like I am missing out when I don't. I thrive on stress (and coffee), and love to stay busy. Whether it's collaborating with passionate and imaginative people, discovering new places to explore, or just watching a movie with my friends, I am always moving forward and always up to something.

Over the next four months, I am planning to develop this website to create a digital masterpiece. As you can tell though, it is still undergoing massive developments so stay tuned for updates!
